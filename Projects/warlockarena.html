WARLOCK ARENA

In my spare time I work with my friend on our own game. Our idea comes from a mod in WC3 called Warlock. It is a multiplayer game where the player shall try to wipe out their opponents for a certain period of time. We use the Unreal Engine 4 engine, and program in C++. The art for the game is not done but most of functionalities are. We have also implemented an AI system with Behavior Trees. The game is still in development.
